Top Accommodations

Discover standout resorts across Mauritius, from beachfront escapes to intimate luxury hideaways, each offering its own mix of comfort, scenery, and island charm.

Top Resorts

Trou aux Biches Beachcomber Golf Resort & Spa

Beachfront resort with many activities, dining options, spa access, and a Kids/Teens Club. Great for families and couples.

Address: Trou-aux-Biches, North Coast, Mauritius

Best for: Families and couples

Constance Belle Mare Plage

Set on a 2km beach with calm water, pools, golf, water sports, and a kids club. Best for families and couples.

Address: Poste de Flacq, Belle Mare, Mauritius

Best for: Families and couples

More info

LUX* Le Morne

Stylish beach-and-mountain resort with spa, wellness, water sports, and romantic experiences. Great for couples and honeymooners.

Address: Le Morne Peninsula, Mauritius

Best for: Couples and honeymooners

More info

The Oberoi Beach Resort

Peaceful luxury villas and pavilions in Turtle Bay with ocean views, a top spa, and wellness-focused activities.

Address: Baie aux Tortues, Pointe aux Piments, Mauritius

Best for: Families, couples, and honeymooners

More info

Shanti Maurice Resort & Spa

Remote beach resort with a large wellness spa, kids club, tennis, and calm non-motorized water activities.

Address: Riviere des Galets, Chemin Grenier, Mauritius

Best for: Couples, honeymooners, and solo travelers

More info

20 Degrees Sud

Boutique Relais & Chateaux hideaway near Grand Baie with intimate suites, spa facilities, and land-and-sea excursions.

Address: Coastal Road, Pointe Malartic, Grand Baie, Mauritius

Best for: Honeymooners and couples
