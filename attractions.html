Top Attractions

Mauritius is packed with unforgettable experiences, from colorful natural wonders and mountain trails to wildlife parks and world-class beaches. Explore this list of top attractions to help plan an adventure-filled island itinerary.

Must-Visit Places

Water Sports

Enjoy snorkeling, kayaking, parasailing, jet-skiingand glass-bottom boat tours and many more activities in calm turquoise waters accross the island.

Seven Colored Earths

Visit the famous Chamarel dunes where natural volcanic sands form layered colors that shift in the sunlight.

Hike through the Black River Gorges National Park

Explore scenic trails, waterfalls, and native forests with panoramic viewpoints across Mauritius' largest protected park.

Le Morne Brabant (Table Mountain)

Hike this UNESCO World Heritage mountain for ocean views, caves, and waterfalls, or relax on the beach below.

La Vanille Nature Park

Meet giant Aldabra tortoises, see Nile crocodiles, feed lemurs and explore the insectarium, aquarium, and fossil museum.

Mauritius Beaches

Enjoy iconic beaches like Trou aux Biches, Ile aux Cerfs, Belle Mare, and Blue Bay for swimming, sunbathing, and water sports.

Pamplemousses Botanical Garden

Visit one of the oldest botanical gardens in the southern hemisphere and walk among exotic palms, lilies, and tropical plants.

Chamarel Waterfalls

See one of Mauritius' most famous waterfalls as it drops through green cliffs into a scenic gorge near Chamarel.

Casela Nature Parks

Spend the day enjoying safari-style wildlife encounters, ziplining, and family-friendly adventure activities at Casela.

Visitor Notes

  • Book your tickets to go immediately, and make an itenirary for your trip.
  • Don't forget to bring your sunscreen and a hat.
  • Make sure to bring your camera to capture the beauty of Mauritius.
